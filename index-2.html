The sense of smell

Dogs have the most sensitive sense of smell among all animals. Blind dogs can use their noses to live like normal dogs. The dog's sensitive sense of smell is mainly manifested in two aspects: one is the degree of sensitivity to smell; The second is the ability to distinguish smells. Sensitivity varies depending on the type of smell. Dogs are 1 million or even 10 million times more capable of detecting odors than humans, and more than 1,000 times more capable of distinguishing odors. They can distinguish about 20,000 different odors. [10]
